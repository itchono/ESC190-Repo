Write a program that:
1. prompts the user for an amount of money
2. purchases items with associated cost in order from this list:
"road bike" 1000
"plane ticket" 200
"second hand bike" 100
"vacuum cleaner" 50
"thrift store jacket" 20
"subway" 5
"cup of coffee" 2
"paper clip" 0.1

3. Prints out the items you purchased in the format.
4. Prints out the remaining amount of money to 2 decimal places.
"You have 0.00 dollars left."

Learning objectives
if statement
